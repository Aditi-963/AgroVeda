## ğŸ’¡ The Problem
Agriculture is the backbone of our economy, but farmers lose **billions of dollars annually** due to undetected crop diseases.
* **Late Detection:** Farmers often notice diseases when it is already too late.
* **Lack of Knowledge:** Identifing specific fungal or bacterial infections requires expert knowledge that isn't always available.
* **Chemical Overuse:** Without knowing the exact disease, farmers often spray wrong or excessive chemicals, harming the soil and consumers.

## ğŸ› ï¸ The Solution: AgroVeda
AgroVeda is an intelligent "Plant Doctor" in your pocket.
1.  **Instant Scanning:** Farmers can simply upload or click a photo of a crop leaf.
2.  **AI Diagnosis:** Our system analyzes the leaf texture and color patterns to detect diseases like *Early Blight*, *Yellow Leaf Curl*, and *Bacterial Spots*.
3.  **Immediate Results:** Within seconds, the user gets a "Healthy" or "Infected" status (Simulated for this MVP).

If you want to run the full Python version on your local machine:


